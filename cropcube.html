CropCube

Problem: some people don't have access to gardening whether they live in a small space, cold climate, or it might be too daunting for them.

Cropcube provides a beautiful and cost effective solution to this, while helping to save the environment. It's an indoor automated hydroponic vertical gardening solution that allows you to grow fresh herbs and veggies from the comfort of your own home, without any harmful pesticides.

My role in the project was to wire all peripherals together and write the software to control them, using a raspberry pi.
